Steak

Description:

Ingredients

  • Ribeye
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat your pan on medium-high heat until smoke arises.
  2. When smoke arises add oil and wait for 1-2 minutes.
  3. While waiting, season steak thoroughly.
  4. After 1-2 minutes passed, place steak in pan for 9 minutes. Flip each minute.
  5. When finished, remove and rest steak for at least 5 minutes and enjoy!
