Lasagna

Description:

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  3. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  4. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
